The life and times of Terry Pratchett

  • 1971 - Published his first novel The Carpet People.
  • 1983 - Published his first Discworld novel The Colour of Magic.
  • 1998 - He was appointed Officer of the Order of the British Empire.
  • 2001 - He won the anual Carnegie Medal for The Amazing Maurice and his Educated Rodents, first Discworld book for children.
  • 2007 - He announced that he had been diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease.
  • 2009 - He was knighted for services to literature.
  • 2010 - He recieved the World Fantasy Award for Life Achievement.
  • 2015 - Terry pratchett died on 12 March at the age of 66.
  • 2015 - Posthumously publish his last novel The Shepherds Crown.
